Categories

To help add structure and encourage us to eat a diversity of stuff, we’ve come up with some categories to try to rotate through:

  • Soup
  • Pasta
  • Veggie main (roughly twice a cycle)
  • Pizza
  • Stew (roughly twice a cycle), with a starchy-side
  • Kiddos choice
  • Build-your-own
  • Bread and cheese night
  • Dinner out (roughly twice a cycle)

We also can break it down by seasonality, though we don’t have a good way to select by dinner category ‚à© season.

  • Spring
  • Summer
  • Fall
  • Winter

When tagging, think:

  • Meal? (lunch/dinner)
  • Main or side? tag: side, veggie-side (assume main is the default)
  • Category?
  • Season?
  • Main ingredient?
  • freezes well? tag: freezer
