Raisin Cream Cheese

Version 1:

  • ½ lb cream cheese
  • 2 juniper handfuls shredded carrot
  • 1 enormous juniper handful raisins
  • 2 juniper handfuls chopped walnuts

Notes: next time we should dice the raisins?
